## Dimensions
### Ink/Stitch Medium
At 100%, Ink/Stitch medium is about  19 mm (3/4 inch) high.
It can be scaled up to 150%  (approx 29 mm, 1 1/8 inch) and downn to 75% (approx. 14 mm, 1/2 inch)
