## Glyphs
 
Cherry for Kaalleen contains 36 glyphs :

## Dimensions

At 100%, this font is approximatively 75 mm high (3 inches)

It can be scaled up to 130% (approx 100 mm, 4 inches ) or scaled down to 80% (approx 60  mm, 2 inches).
